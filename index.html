Section 7

Frequently asked questions

A few quick answers to the most common questions teams ask before they start managed certificate monitoring with SecureOrbit.

Is CertWatch self-service or managed?

CertWatch is a managed service. SecureOrbit operates the monitoring, reviews findings internally, and sends your team alerts, monthly reports, and recommended next steps.

Do clients get a dashboard?

No client dashboard is required. We run the console internally so your team gets the outcomes without needing another portal to learn or manage.

How often are reports sent?

Reports are sent monthly, with alerts delivered in between whenever certificate issues, discoveries, or coverage changes need attention.

What happens when a certificate is close to expiration?

We alert your team before the expiration window becomes urgent, include the issue in reporting, and provide recommendations on the renewal owner and next action.

Can you monitor multiple domains and discovered subdomains?

Yes. CertWatch can monitor multiple domains across your scope and surface newly discovered certificate activity, including relevant subdomains observed through certificate transparency data.
